What Is The Way?

The Way is the original path of worship and obedience established by Yahweh from the beginning—long before modern religion, denominations, or church systems existed. In Scripture, The Way is not a new movement and not a man-made tradition, but the covenant path revealed to the patriarchs, given to Israel through the Torah, and lived perfectly by Yeshua the Messiah. The earliest followers of Yeshua were known as followers of The Way, a people who believed the Torah and the Prophets and ordered their lives according to Yahweh’s instructions.

The question remains: will you search this out in Scripture and walk the way Yahweh established from the beginning?
